RECOGNITION OF TYPEWRITING AND SHORTHAND INSTITUTIONS 

IN THE STATE OF ANDHRA PRADESH 

 

  1. A notification will be placed along with application form in the websites of DTE, AP and SBTET, AP in the month of June/July every year inviting applications from the Principals of Typewriting and Shorthand Institutions for grant of recognition/renewal of recognition.
  2. The recognition is awarded for one year (calendar year) and two years block period. 

 

  1. The institutions recognised by the Director/Commissioner of Technical Education, A.P., are only eligible to sponsor candidates for the Typewriting and Shorthand Examinations conducted by State Board of Technical Education and Training, Andhra Pradesh. The SBTET, AP, conduct Typewriting and Shorthand Exams twice in a year. 

 

NORMS AND GUIDELINES FOR RECOGNITION OF INSTITUTIONS

 

  1. The institution seeking recognition shall have a minimum of (5) computers or a combination of Computers and English Typewriters. If coaching is given in Telugu/Hindi/Urdu also, there shall be at least one typewriter each in Telugu/Hindi/Urdu, as the case may be.
  2. The institute should have on its rolls a minimum of (15) students in English Typewriting and if coaching is given in Telugu/Hindi/Urdu also, there shall be (5) students each in Telugu/Hindi/Urdu, as the case may be.
  3. For seeking fresh recognition (newly established Institute), the Principal of the Institute should be qualified by possessing Intermediate or equivalent qualification and Typewriting/Shorthand Higher Grade in any language and should have (2) years’ experience after passing Higher Grade Examination in Typewriting or Shorthand, as the case may be. They should maintain a distance of at least one furlong from the recognized Institute existing, if any, in the area to avoid unhealthy competition.

 

  1. For seeking two year block period recognition, the institute shall have (5) years or more years of standing and in receipt of recognition orders for (5) years in favour of the same Principal.
  2. The Principals of Typewriting and Shorthand Institutes shall not shift the Institutes from the place of its original sanction. If the shifting is inevitable, they should seek permission of the Department before shifting and any deviation from these guidelines shall be liable for cancellation of the recognition.
  3. In case of change of Principal of any recognized Institute without the change of premises of the Institute, it will be treated as fresh recognition. In such case, the new Principal should have the qualifications as stated in para (3) of these guidelines. The attested xerox copies of the educational & technical qualifications of the Principal/instructors enclosed to the filled-in application.

PAYMENT OF RECOGNITION FEE

 

  1. The fee towards renewal/fresh recognition is Rs.1000/- per annum. The penal fee for late submission of filled application is Rs.500/- (in addition to recognition fee) as per G.O.Rt.No.103, Skills Development and Training (TE.A1) Department, dated 25-08-2022.

RECEIPT OF APPLICATIONS:

  1. The filled in applications, with Treasury Challan, in original, and attested copies of necessary enclosures shall be sent superscribing on the cover “Application for seeking fresh/renewal of recognition of Commercial and Technical Institutes” and addressed to the Commissioner of Technical Education, 1st   floor,  Garudadri K.K. Towers, Near Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Colony Bye-Pass, Mangalagiri, Guntur District – 522 503.  The applications should reach this office on or before the dates stipulated in the notification from time to time.

 

 

  1. The applications found to be incomplete, defective or not in accordance with the guidelines prescribed for renewal/fresh recognition will be summarily rejected and no further correspondence will be entertained in this regard. Further, the Department of Technical Education is not responsible for late receipt of applications due to postal delay or loss of application in transit, etc.

 

      INSPECTION OF INSTITUTIONS: 

As per the instructions of the CTE, AP, the Principal of the nearby Government Polytechnic shall conduct the field inspection of Type and Shorthand Institutions allotted to him/her as per the Guidelines/Inspection Format and submit a report to the Commissioner of Technical Education, A.P. 

 

GRANT OF RECOGNITION:

After thorough verification and scrutiny of the applications and inspection reports submitted by the field inspection officers, the Commissioner of Technical Education grant the recognition/renewal of recognition to the eligible Institutions.
