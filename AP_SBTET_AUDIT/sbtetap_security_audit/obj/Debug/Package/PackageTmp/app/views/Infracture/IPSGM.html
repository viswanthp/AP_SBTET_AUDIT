IPSGM

The department firmly believes that nurturing physically, mentally, emotionally, and a socially competent individual leads to real-world success and happiness. To support this belief, the department actively promotes physical activities.
In line with its commitment, the Department of Technical Education organizes the INTER POLYTECHNIC SPORTS AND GAMES MEET (IPSGM) annually. The IPSGM serves as a platform that offers abundant opportunities for students to engage in physical fitness, skill development, teamwork, sportsmanship, mental well-being, social interaction, spirit, and personal growth. This year, 2022-23, the IPSGM at regional level is conducted in all the 13 districts of Andhra Pradesh during January, 2023. Following that, the state-level IPSGM event is organized at Govt. Polytechnic, Vijayawada in the month of February, 2023. And students from various polytechnics have participated in various sports and made the event grand successful.
