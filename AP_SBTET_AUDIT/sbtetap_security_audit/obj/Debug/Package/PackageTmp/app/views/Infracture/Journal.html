Journal

Journals play a crucial role in the academic and research community by providing a platform for researchers to share their findings, theories, methodologies, and insights with their peers and the broader scholarly community. They serve as a means of communication, dissemination, and documentation of scholarly research and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in specific fields.
They provide an opportunity to the staff and students of various Polytechnics , to publish their articles, projects and innovative ideas in the form of essays.
They are often peer-reviewed, articles undergo rigorous evaluation by experts before they are published. Journals serve as a platform for researchers, scholars, and professionals to share their original research, analysis, and insights with the wider community.
Overall, journals are crucial for the advancement of knowledge, quality control, and the development of academic and professional communities.
