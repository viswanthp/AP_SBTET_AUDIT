STATE BOARD OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING

MANGALAGIRI, ANDHRA PRADESH

 

GRANTING AFFILIATION TO PRIVATE COLLEGES OFFERING DIPLOMA COURSES BY THE STATE BOARD OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING,  

ANDHRA PRADESH

 

  1. All Private Polytechnics/ Private Engineering Colleges Offering Diploma Courses in the State of Andhra Pradesh must be affiliated to the STATE BOARD OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING ANDHRA PRADESH for Every Academic Year.
  2. The Private Polytechnics/ Private Engineering Colleges Offering Diploma Courses in the State of Andhra Pradesh seeking affiliation to their Institutes / Colleges should apply in the prescribed application format as per the SBTET norms.  
  3. The Application Procedure is now made ONLINE. The institutes should apply through their “Management Information System (MIS)” Login Credentials.  
  4. A notification will be placed in the SBTE&T, A.P Web Site inviting applications from the concerned Principles for Grant of Affiliation well before the starting of admission process every year by the SBTE&T, A.P
  5. Basing on the applications received, SBTE&T , A.P shall appoint inspection committees consisting both administrative and subject wise experts for physical Verification in their institutes.   
  6. The Institutes should produce all relevant documents, proofs, records, photographs etc. as per the inspection format before the Inspection Committee. 
  7. The faculty members of the concerned institutes should present physically at the time of inspection.
  8. The inspection committee will submit a detailed report basing on the institute application and physical verification to SBTE&T, A.P.
  9. The Inspection Officers shall submit their inspection report through MIS using their Login Credentials. 
  10. As per the recommendations of the Inspection Committee, the Secretary, SBTE&T, A.P., will Grant Affiliation or Issue notices to concerned institutes to comply the deficiencies in the prescribed time.
