Interim Certificate

Procedure for Applying for Interim Certificate:

A interim certificate is a certificate that educational institutions provide when a student leaves the organization after completed the diploma, student submit the same to the University authorities / Recruitment agencies for prosecuting higher studies /employment in other states /countries.

To get Interim Certificate, Submit  an application downloaded from the button given below, fill it and address it to The Secretary, State Board of Technical Education and Training, #11-307, Garudadri KK   Towers, Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Colony, Service Road, Mangalagiri, Guntur – 522503.

 

 

Documents to be submitted (either through post or in person)

 

  1. Filled in application form, which is downloaded from  https://sbtetap.gov.in/Screens/EligibilityandForms.aspx

 

  1.  
  1. Manual Procedure:  (Attach Demand Draft) 

 

Demand Draft  drawn in favor of The SECRETARY, SBTET. ANDHRA PRADESH  for Rs.600/- for each migration certificate.

Migration Certificate

Procedure for Applying for Migration  Certificate:

A migration certificate is a certificate that educational institutions provide when a student leaves the organization. When a student transfers from one institution to another or from one board to another for higher education, this certificate is necessary. Without it, student cannot continue their education beyond Diploma.

To get Migration Certificate, Submit  an application downloaded from the button given below, fill it and address it to The Secretary, State Board of Technical Education and Training, #11-307, Garudadri KK   Towers, Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Colony, Service Road, Mangalagiri, Guntur – 522503.

Transcripts

Procedure for Applying for Transcripts Certificate:

A transcript certificate is nothing but a document that acts as proof of your educational qualifications. It includes the complete record of all the subjects studied by the students in his/her academic session along with marks or grades respectively. 

To get Transcripts Certificate, Submit  an application downloaded from the button given below, fill it and address it to The Secretary, State Board of Technical Education and Training, #11-307, Garudadri KK   Towers Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Colony, Service Road,Mangalagiri,Guntur – 522503. 

 

Documents to be submitted (either through post or in person).

 

  1. Filled in application form, which is downloaded from  https://sbtetap.gov.in/Screens/EligibilityandForms.aspx
