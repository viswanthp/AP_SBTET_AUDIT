Industrial Training

The purpose of industrial training is to provide students with hands-on experience, exposure to industry practices, and an opportunity to apply their knowledge in a professional environment. It helps students develop practical skills, gain industry insights.
The main objectives of Industrial Training are to:
1.Expose to real time working environment 2.Enhance knowledge and skill already learnt in the institution 3.Acquire new skills related to Industry practices 4.Develop qualities like team & work culture, integrity, responsibility
Six months mandatory Industrial Training provides an opportunity to gain practical experience and to apply the knowledge acquired in academics. It offers a platform to develop various Technical and soft skills such as communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and time management which will boost the students employability. It is a valuable stepping stone towards a successful and fulfilling career.
