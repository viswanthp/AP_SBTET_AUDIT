Certificate Services

The objective of Certificate Services is to Provides services to the students to get Interim Certificate, Migration Certificate, Name Correction Certificate, Transcripts, Duplicate Mark Memos and Duplicate Certificates and Equivalency Certificate...etc.

Question Papers

The objective of the Question Papers Service is to provide a away to download previous examinations Question Papers to make the student familiar with examination pattern. Here they can find old question papers and their patterns of schemes C20, C16, C14, C09...etc.

Syllabus

The objective of this Syllabus Service is to provide a way to download Curriculum of various schemes to make the student familiar with advancement of various schemes like (C23, C20, C16, C14, C09...etc) framing the Syllabus.

Time Tables

The objective of Time Tables service is to inform the students the examination notifications, various timeline of exam fee payment and to download exam schedule of various schemes. Exam Timetables helps maintain order, fairness, and efficiency during the examination period.

Exam Fee Payment

The objective of this service is to make the students to pay the examination fee with respective to the current notification. These payment gateways allow students to pay their exam fees using credit cards, debit cards, net banking, or other digital payment methods.

Hall Ticket

The objective of this service is to download hall tickets to admit them into the examination Hall tickets serve as a means of identification for the students. They typically contain essential information such as the student's name, photograph, roll number...etc.

Results

The objective of this service is to make the students to download the results of various schemes & semesters. Exam results play a crucial role in determining whether a student has fulfilled all the necessary requirements to get promoted to next semester and finally to receive Diploma Certificate.
