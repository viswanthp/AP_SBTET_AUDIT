Tech Fest

One of the initiatives taken by the Department of Technical Education is the organization of POLY TECHFEST, which serves as a remarkable platform for showcasing the exceptional talent of young individuals in a creative and innovative manner. It provides participants with a valuable opportunity to unleash their scientific excellence and demonstrate their unique abilities. By offering this platform, the fest encourages participants to push their boundaries, explore new ideas, and showcase their remarkable achievements.The first successful POLY TECHFESTwas conducted in 2018, and it has since become a valuable opportunity for students in the state.
This year, the Regional Poly Techfestwas successfully organized in all 13 districts of Andhra Pradesh, providing a platform for polytechnic students to showcase their skills and talents. The State-level POLY TECHFESTwas held at Vijayawada, attracting participants from all corners of the state of Andhra Pradesh.
