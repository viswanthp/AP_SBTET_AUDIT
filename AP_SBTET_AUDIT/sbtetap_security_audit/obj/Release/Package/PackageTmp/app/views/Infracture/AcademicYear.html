Academic Year

These calendars provide a framework for planning and organizing academic activities, including class schedules, examinations, holidays, registration periods, and other significant events.
Academic calendars are typically published and distributed by the Diploma colleges well in advance, allowing students, faculty, and staff to plan their academic activities, vacations, and other commitments. It serves as a guide to ensure the smooth running of the academic year and to maintain consistency in scheduling across the year.
Academic calendars assist Polytechnics in planning and allocating resources effectively. By having a clear schedule of the academic year, Polytechnics can plan workload, and resources, coordinate support services, and ensure efficient use of facilities and equipment.
Academic calendars highlight important events and deadlines within the academic year. These may include faculty meetings, departmental seminars, conferences..etc.
