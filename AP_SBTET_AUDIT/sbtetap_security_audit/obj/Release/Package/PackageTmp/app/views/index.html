About SBTET

STATE BOARD OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING (SBTET) was formed vide G.O.Ms.No.371, Edn, dated. 26.2.1957. It is formed as a statutory body under the A.P. Education Act-1982 (Act 1of 1982), an act of assembly, in the present form, vide G.O.Ms.No.140, Dated24.4.1984. AP State Board of Technical Education and Training was formed on 18th June, 2015 after bifurcation.

The SBTET shall continue its pursuit to attain excellence and work with great effort to safeguard and guarantee the delivery and impart of quality, career and job oriented technical education to the energetic youth of Andhra Pradesh.

  • To frame policies for Technical Education
  • To review and revise the syllabi as per requirements of the society
  • To supervise the teaching in technical institutions
  • To conduct examinations and award Certificates
  • To affiliate educational institutions and accord recognition

VISION

SBTET is committed to Transparency, Accuracy, Integrity, Innovation and Societal Relevance in realizing human resource potential as International Outlook professionals in Technical Education and Responsible Citizens.

MISSION

To advise the Government on the coordinate development of Technical Education in the state at below under-graduate To work in liaison with the Southern Regional Committee of the AICTE in the formulation of schemes in the state Developing Govt. Institutions, guiding and supervising the aided, private institutions Interacting with industry and national level institutions, co-ordinating with them for quality improvement Conducting Examinations/valuation/announcement of results and issue of certificates to Diploma and other Certificate courses as per schedule with Transparency Conducting Staff development training programs in coordination with NITTTR (National Institute of Technical Teachers Training & Research)

    What We Do

    Affiliation

    The Council will consider proposals for starting second shift enrollment initiatives for optimal utilization of infrastructure facilities. The institutions will be required to provide teaching faculty and the Head of the Institution separately for the second shift. This provision will be considered for both new as well as existing institutions.

    Infrastructure

    Infrastructure plays an important role in education sector. Buildings, classrooms, laboratories, and equipment are crucial elements of a learning environment.
    There is strong evidence that high-quality infrastructure facilitates better instruction, improves student outcomes, and reduces dropout rates, among other benefits.

    Placements

    The placement cell is continuously making efforts for the students to get the requisite skills. Efforts are being made to call the experts from various fields in order to develop their personality, generate the soft skills, building up of self-confidence and develop the employment skills amongst the students.
