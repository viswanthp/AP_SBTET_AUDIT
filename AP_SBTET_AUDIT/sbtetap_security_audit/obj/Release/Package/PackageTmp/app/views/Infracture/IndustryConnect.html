Industry Connect

The purpose of Industry Connect is to enhance the practical relevance and real-world applicability of academic programs, research, and student experiences. It provides opportunities for students, faculty, and institutions to interact with industry professionals, gain industry insights, and apply theoretical knowledge in practical settings. Similarly, industries benefit from this Connect by accessing new talent, research collaborations, and staying updated on the latest academic developments in their field.
Industry Connect facilitates the exchange of knowledge, fosters innovation, and promotes a practical understanding of industry requirements. It helps students develop industry-relevant skills, enhances their employability, and creates a mutually beneficial relationship between student and industries. With relevant skills and familiarity with the industry activities it would be very beneficial for students in real time and to acquire jobs.
